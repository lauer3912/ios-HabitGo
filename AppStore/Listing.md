## description

HabitGo is your personal habit tracker designed to help you build lasting habits and achieve your goals. Simple, beautiful, and effective — no complexity, just results.

### Why HabitGo?

**Build Better Habits**
Track daily habits with a single tap. See your progress at a glance and stay accountable every day.

**Streaks Keep You Motivated**
Watch your streak grow as you complete habits. Streaks create accountability and make habit building feel rewarding.

**Beautiful & Simple**
Clean design that gets out of your way. Add habits in seconds and start tracking immediately.

**Track Your Progress**
View detailed statistics: current streaks, longest streaks, and total completions. Know exactly how you're doing.

**Fully Private**
All your data stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no tracking. Your habits are yours alone.

### Features

- Track unlimited daily habits
- Visual streak tracking with flame indicators
- Multiple frequency options: daily, weekdays, weekends, weekly
- Beautiful color-coded icons
- Progress overview on the home screen
- All-time statistics dashboard
- No account or login required
- No internet connection required
- 100% private — data never leaves your device

### Privacy

HabitGo stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. No analytics, no advertising, no third-party SDKs.
