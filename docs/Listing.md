## Description

Build lasting habits and achieve your goals with HabitArcFlow.

HabitArcFlow is your personal habit tracker designed to help you build lasting habits and achieve your goals. Simple, beautiful, and effective -- no complexity, just results.

Why HabitArcFlow?

- Build Better Habits -- Track daily habits with a single tap. See your progress at a glance and stay accountable every day.

- Streaks Keep You Motivated -- Watch your streak grow as you complete habits. Streaks create accountability and make habit building feel rewarding.

- Beautiful and Simple -- Clean design that gets out of your way. Add habits in seconds and start tracking immediately.

- Track Your Progress -- View detailed statistics: current streaks, longest streaks, and total completions. Know exactly how you're doing.

- Multiple Frequencies -- Set habits as daily, weekdays, weekends, or weekly. Track at your own pace.

- Visual Calendar -- See your habit history on a monthly calendar. Know exactly which days you succeeded and which days you missed.

- Fully Private -- All your data stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no tracking. Your habits are yours alone.

Features

- Track unlimited daily habits
- Visual streak tracking with flame indicators
- Multiple frequency options: daily, weekdays, weekends, weekly
- Beautiful color-coded icons
- Progress overview on the home screen
- All-time statistics dashboard
- Monthly calendar view
- No account or login required
- No internet connection required
- 100% private -- data never leaves your device
